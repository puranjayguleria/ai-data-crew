## 1. Setting Up Your First Ad Campaign

### Step-by-Step Setup
1. Go to **Meta Business Suite** → Ads Manager
2. Click **+ Create** (green button, top left)
3. Choose your campaign objective (see Section 2)
4. Set your daily budget (start with ₹200-500/day)
5. Define your audience (see Section 3)
6. Upload your creative + write your ad copy
7. Review and publish

### Account Checklist Before You Start
- [ ] Facebook Business Page created
- [ ] Instagram Business Account linked
- [ ] Meta Pixel installed on your website
- [ ] Payment method added
- [ ] Business verification completed (for higher limits)

## 2. Choosing the Right Campaign Objective

| Your Goal | Choose This Objective | Best For |
|-----------|----------------------|----------|
| Brand awareness | **Awareness** | New businesses, launches |
| Website visitors | **Traffic** | Blog posts, landing pages |
| Leads / enquiries | **Leads** | Service businesses |
| Sales / purchases | **Sales** | E-commerce, digital products |
| App installs | **App Promotion** | Mobile apps |
| Messages on WhatsApp | **Engagement** | Local businesses |

### Pro Tip
> Start with **Traffic** or **Leads** campaigns. They're cheapest to test and give you data fast. Move to Sales only after you know what creative + audience works.

## 3. Audience Targeting (The Secret Sauce)

### Tier 1: Interest-Based Targeting (Easiest)
Pick 3-5 interests relevant to your product/service:

**Example for a bakery:**
- Interest: Baking, Cake decorating, Food & Wine
- Age: 25-55
- Gender: All
- Location: Your city + 25km radius

**Example for a gym:**
- Interest: Fitness, Yoga, Weight loss, CrossFit
- Age: 18-45
- Location: Your city + 10km radius

### Tier 2: Lookalike Audiences (Best ROI)
1. Upload your customer list (emails/phones) as a Custom Audience
2. Create a 1% Lookalike from it
3. This finds people similar to your best customers
4. Minimum 100 customers needed for good results

### Tier 3: Retargeting (Highest Conversion)
- Website visitors (last 30 days)
- People who engaged with your Instagram/Facebook
- People who watched 50%+ of your video ads
- Cart abandoners (for e-commerce)

### Audience Size Guide
| Budget/Day | Ideal Audience Size |
|------------|-------------------|
| ₹200-500 | 50,000 - 200,000 |
| ₹500-1,000 | 200,000 - 500,000 |
| ₹1,000-3,000 | 500,000 - 2,000,000 |
| ₹3,000+ | 1,000,000+ |

## 5. Budget Planning & Bidding Strategy

### The 70-20-10 Rule
- **70%** of budget → Your best-performing ad set (proven audience + creative)
- **20%** of budget → Testing new audiences
- **10%** of budget → Testing new creatives

### Monthly Budget Planner

| Monthly Budget | Daily Spend | Expected Reach/Day | Expected Clicks/Day |
|---------------|-------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| ₹6,000 | ₹200 | 1,000-3,000 | 20-50 |
| ₹15,000 | ₹500 | 3,000-8,000 | 50-150 |
| ₹30,000 | ₹1,000 | 7,000-15,000 | 100-300 |
| ₹90,000 | ₹3,000 | 20,000-50,000 | 300-800 |

### Bidding Tips
- Start with **Lowest Cost** (automatic) bidding
- Never change budget by more than 20% at a time
- Wait 3-5 days before judging performance
- If CPC > ₹15 for traffic campaigns, change the creative

## 6. Creative Best Practices

### Image Ads
- Use **1:1 ratio** (1080x1080px) for feed
- Use **9:16 ratio** (1080x1920px) for Stories/Reels
- Less than 20% text on the image
- Use bright, contrasting colors
- Show the product in use (not just the product)
- Include your logo (small, corner)

### Video Ads
- Hook in the first 3 seconds
- Keep under 30 seconds for Stories
- Keep under 60 seconds for Feed
- Add captions (80% watch without sound)
- End with a clear CTA

### Carousel Ads
- Use 3-5 slides
- Slide 1: Hook / Problem
- Slides 2-4: Solution / Benefits
- Last Slide: CTA + Offer

## 7. Tracking & Optimization Checklist

### Weekly Optimization Routine
- [ ] Check CTR (Click-Through Rate) — target > 1.5%
- [ ] Check CPC (Cost Per Click) — target < ₹10
- [ ] Check CPL (Cost Per Lead) — depends on your business
- [ ] Turn off ads with CTR < 0.8% after 1,000 impressions
- [ ] Increase budget on winners by 20%
- [ ] Refresh creatives every 2 weeks
- [ ] Check frequency — pause if > 3.0

### Red Flags to Watch
| Metric | Red Flag | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| CTR | Below 0.8% | Change creative or audience |
| CPC | Above ₹20 | Narrow audience or change ad |
| Frequency | Above 3.0 | Audience is too small or ad is stale |
| Relevance Score | Below 5/10 | Complete creative overhaul needed |
| CPL | 3x your target | Pause and rethink funnel |
