Content Writer Job Application Pack

This sample pack is built for clients hiring freelance or remote content writers. It shows practical writing range across SEO, social media, and conversion-focused business content.

What’s included

  • SEO blog writing sample
  • Instagram caption strategy sample
  • Homepage copy improvement sample
  • Short-form promotional copy sample

Sample 1: SEO Blog Intro

Most small businesses do not need more content. They need content that matches search intent, builds trust fast, and moves readers toward inquiry. A strong SEO article is not just keyword placement. It is structure, clarity, relevance, and a clear next step.

Sample 2: Instagram Caption

Still posting every day but getting no DMs? Your visuals may be fine, but your caption may be doing nothing. A better caption gives context, builds trust, and tells the customer why they should care right now. Save this if you run a local business and want content that actually converts.

Sample 3: Homepage Copy Rewrite

Before: We provide quality digital solutions for all your business needs.

After: We help local businesses get more calls, bookings, and repeat customers with sharper website copy, clearer offers, and content that makes people trust you faster.

Sample 4: Promo Copy

Launching a summer offer? Do not just announce the discount. Frame the benefit. Example: “Beat the slow season with 15 ready-to-post captions designed for Indian local businesses that want faster posting and better engagement without hiring a full-time marketer.”
