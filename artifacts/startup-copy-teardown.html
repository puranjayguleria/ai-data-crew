3 mistakes that kill startup conversions

1. Vague headline

If your headline sounds like every other SaaS site, users will not understand the product fast enough to care.

2. Weak CTA

If the homepage says “Learn more” instead of pushing a demo, signup, or trial, intent gets lost.

3. Proof is buried

If trust signals, outcomes, or credibility appear too late, visitors bounce before confidence is built.
