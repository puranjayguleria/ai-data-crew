MINI SEO AUDIT SAMPLE

Business Type: Local Service Business

1. Homepage headline is too generic
Current messaging does not clearly mention the main service or target city. Recommendation: rewrite the H1 to include service + city for stronger relevance and conversion.

2. Weak internal linking
Important service pages are not prominently linked from the homepage. Recommendation: add clear internal links to top service pages in hero, body copy, and footer.

3. Missing trust signals
The page lacks visible review snippets, FAQs, and proof elements. Recommendation: add customer reviews, service guarantees, and location-specific FAQs to improve both trust and search performance.

4. Google Business Profile opportunity
Business should strengthen category selection, service descriptions, and image freshness. Recommendation: update profile weekly and align services with website copy.

5. Quick-win priority order
First: homepage headline rewrite
Second: service page internal links
Third: review and FAQ block
These three changes usually produce the fastest improvement in local relevance and lead quality.
