3 AD IDEAS FOR LOCAL BUSINESSES

1. SALON OFFER AD
Headline: First Visit Offer — Hair Spa / Cleanup / Styling Discount
Angle: New customer acquisition
CTA: Message on WhatsApp to book this week

2. GYM CHALLENGE AD
Headline: Join Our 21-Day Fitness Challenge
Angle: Short commitment, visible result, local convenience
CTA: Claim your trial session today

3. COACHING CENTER AD
Headline: Limited Seats for New Monthly Batch
Angle: Parent pain point + score improvement + structured learning
CTA: Ask for syllabus and demo class on WhatsApp

How to use:
- Keep radius targeting local
- Use one clear offer only
- Send traffic to WhatsApp or a simple landing page
- Start with ₹100-₹300/day for testing
