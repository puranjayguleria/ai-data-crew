HOW TO RUN FACEBOOK ADS ON ₹100/DAY FOR SMALL BUSINESSES

Price: ₹249

Who this is for:
Local businesses, solo founders, coaches, cafes, salons, boutiques, tutors, and service providers who want leads without wasting money.

SECTION 1: THE TRUTH ABOUT ₹100/DAY ADS
A ₹100/day budget will not build a giant brand overnight. But it can absolutely help a small business test offers, identify winning creatives, and generate first leads when used with discipline. The goal is not scale on day one. The goal is signal: what message gets attention, what audience clicks, and what offer makes people act.

The biggest mistake small businesses make is boosting random posts. A better approach is to run one focused campaign with one business goal. If you are a salon, promote a first-visit offer. If you are a tutor, promote a free demo class. If you are a restaurant, promote a combo meal or weekday deal. Small budgets only work when the offer is specific.

SECTION 2: PICK ONE GOAL
Choose only one objective for your first 7 days:
1. WhatsApp inquiries
2. Lead form submissions
3. Website visits to one offer page

For most local businesses in India, WhatsApp is the easiest conversion path. Customers are already comfortable messaging. That means your ad should promise one clear outcome and push users to start a chat.

SECTION 3: THE BEST OFFERS FOR LOW BUDGET ADS
Good low-budget offers:
- Free consultation
- First order discount
- Limited-time combo
- Free trial/demo
- Free audit/checkup
- Buy 1 get 1 weekday deal

Weak offers:
- "We are the best in town"
- "Contact us for more"
- Generic awareness ads with no reason to click

SECTION 4: AUDIENCE SETUP
Start narrow, not broad.
Example setups:
- Restaurant: 3-5 km radius around location, age 18-40
- Salon: 5-8 km radius, women/men depending on service
- Tutor: parents or students in city, age filtered by decision-maker
- Real estate broker: one city, one property type, one budget bracket if possible

Do not stack too many interests. For a ₹100/day campaign, location + simple demographic targeting is often enough.

SECTION 5: CREATIVE FORMULA
Use this 3-part ad creative formula:
HOOK + OFFER + ACTION

Examples:
- "Hungry after work? Get our weekday combo at a special price. Message now to order."
- "Need Class 10 maths support? Book a free demo session this week on WhatsApp."
- "First salon visit? Get 20% off your haircut and cleanup. Limited slots this weekend."

Image/video tips:
- Use one bright image
- Show the product/service clearly
- Add 3-6 words of text on image if needed
- Avoid clutter

SECTION 6: COPY TEMPLATES
Template 1:
Struggling with [problem]? We help [audience] get [result]. Book/message today for [offer].

Template 2:
[City] customers: for this week only, get [offer]. Tap to claim on WhatsApp.

Template 3:
If you need [service], don’t wait. We’re offering [benefit] for [time period]. Message now.

SECTION 7: LANDING DESTINATION
Your ad must go to one of these:
- WhatsApp chat link
- Simple landing page with one CTA
- Lead form

Do not send traffic to a messy homepage with too many options.

SECTION 8: 7-DAY TEST PLAN
Day 1-2: Launch 1 campaign, 2 creatives
Day 3: Pause the weaker creative
Day 4-5: Rewrite headline if CTR is low
Day 6: Improve offer if clicks are okay but no leads
Day 7: Duplicate winner and continue

SECTION 9: METRICS THAT MATTER
Track these:
- CTR (click-through rate)
- Cost per click
- Number of inquiries
- Cost per inquiry
- Sales closed from inquiries

A cheap click means nothing if nobody buys. The real metric is cost per qualified lead or sale.

SECTION 10: COMMON FAILURES
- Too many audiences
- Weak offer
- No CTA
- Sending to homepage
- Running ad for too short a time
- Changing everything every day

SECTION 11: READY-TO-USE MINI AD EXAMPLES
Restaurant:
"Midweek cravings? Grab our special combo today. Fast service, great taste, limited-time price. Tap to order on WhatsApp."

Tutor:
"Board exams coming up? Book a free demo maths class this week. Clear concepts, structured practice, better confidence. Message now."

Salon:
"Fresh look for the weekend. Book your first appointment today and get a special new-client discount. Limited slots available."

SECTION 12: FINAL ACTION PLAN
1. Pick one offer
2. Pick one audience
3. Create two simple creatives
4. Send traffic to WhatsApp or one landing page
5. Run for 7 days
6. Kill losers fast
7. Scale winner slowly
