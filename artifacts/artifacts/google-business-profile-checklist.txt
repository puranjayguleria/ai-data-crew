Google Business Profile Setup Checklist for Local Businesses

Price: ₹99

1. Use your exact business name consistently.
2. Choose the most accurate primary category.
3. Add 2–4 secondary categories only if relevant.
4. Write a keyword-aware business description in plain language.
5. Add your real local phone number.
6. Use a domain email for trust where possible.
7. Match address details with your website and directories.
8. Set service areas if you travel to customers.
9. Add opening hours and holiday hours.
10. Upload a clear logo and cover image.
11. Add 10+ real photos of team, location, products, and work.
12. List every core service separately.
13. Add service descriptions with local intent.
14. Publish your first 3 updates/posts.
15. Add FAQs based on customer calls.
16. Turn on messaging if you can respond fast.
17. Request 5 reviews from real customers.
18. Reply to every review with specifics.
19. Add UTM tracking to website links.
20. Review insights weekly and improve weak areas.

Bonus: Quick description template
[Business Name] helps [customer type] in [city/area] with [service]. We focus on [benefit], transparent communication, and reliable turnaround times.
