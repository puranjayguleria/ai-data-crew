Salon Instagram Leads Mini Pack

This pack is designed for salons that want more bookings from Instagram using stronger offer-led content, proof, and story conversion tactics.

Post 1

Before/After Transformation Reel

Show a real client transformation with a 3-second hook, service name, stylist credit, and CTA: “DM GLOW for this look.”

Post 2

Limited-Time Offer Graphic

Promote a weekday haircut + blowdry combo with a clear expiry date to create urgency and fill low-demand slots.

Post 3

Client Testimonial Carousel

Use 3 short testimonials with photos, service names, and one final booking CTA slide linked to WhatsApp or DM.

Story

Story Poll for Booking Intent

Story copy: “Thinking about a hair refresh this week?” Poll options: “Yes, book me” / “Show me offers”.
