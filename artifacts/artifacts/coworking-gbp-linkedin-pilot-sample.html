Coworking GBP + LinkedIn Pilot Sample

A sample 24-hour pilot for coworking brands that want stronger local visibility and sharper decision-maker content.

3 Google Business Profile Posts

  1. Workspace That Supports Focus: A productive workday starts with a space designed for focus, comfort, and reliability. Flexible workspaces with strong infrastructure help teams and founders work without friction.
  2. Professional Meeting Experience: Client meetings feel different in a workspace that is accessible, well-designed, and business-ready. A polished environment supports stronger first impressions.
  3. Flexible Options for Growing Teams: As teams grow, workspace needs change quickly. Flexible office solutions make it easier to scale without long-term operational stress.

3 LinkedIn Posts

  1. For founders: The right workspace does more than provide desks. It shapes energy, focus, and the quality of conversations your team has every day.
  2. For HR/workplace teams: A good office experience is not just about square footage. It affects employee comfort, collaboration, and how people feel about showing up to work.
  3. For enterprise buyers: Flexibility, professionalism, and speed matter when evaluating workspace solutions. The best environments reduce friction instead of adding it.

3 Review Reply Templates

  1. Thank you for your feedback. We’re glad the workspace and overall experience supported your work well.
  2. We appreciate your review and are happy to know the environment, facilities, and team made a positive impression.
  3. Thank you for sharing your thoughts. We value the feedback and are always working to improve the member experience.
