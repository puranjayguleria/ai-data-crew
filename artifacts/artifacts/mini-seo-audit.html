Mini SEO Audit Sample for a Small E-commerce Homepage

Page Focus

Homepage review for an Indian D2C skincare store.

Top 5 Fixes

  1. Rewrite title tag: Replace generic homepage title with a keyword-rich title focused on the main product category and customer intent.
  2. Add a stronger H1: Use a clear headline that states what the store sells and who it is for.
  3. Improve hero copy: Add 40-60 words explaining benefits, ingredients, and brand trust signals.
  4. Add internal links: Link from homepage blocks to top collections and bestsellers.
  5. Strengthen trust elements: Add shipping, returns, reviews, and secure checkout messaging above the fold.

Expected Outcome

These changes can improve clarity, relevance, and conversion readiness while strengthening basic on-page SEO.
