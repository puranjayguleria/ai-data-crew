Short Formula Templates

A. [Degree/Role] | Interested in [Target Roles] | Skilled in [2-3 Relevant Skills]

Usage Tip
Choose a headline that matches the exact role you are applying for. Do not use a vague headline like 'Student looking for opportunities'. Specific headlines perform better because recruiters can instantly understand your fit.
