SEO Audit Checklist for Small Businesses
Price: ₹99

1. Homepage title tag includes primary service + city
2. Meta description explains benefit and includes CTA
3. One clear H1 on each page
4. Service pages target one service each
5. Contact page includes phone, email, address, and map
6. Google Business Profile linked from website
7. Same business name/address/phone across website and listings
8. Pages load fast on mobile
9. Images compressed and named descriptively
10. Internal links connect homepage to service pages
11. Each service page includes FAQs
12. Add customer reviews/testimonials on key pages
13. Add schema where relevant (LocalBusiness, FAQ)
14. Check indexing in Google Search Console
15. Fix broken links and 404 pages
16. Add city/location modifiers naturally in copy
17. Publish at least 3 local intent blog posts
18. Use clear CTAs above the fold
19. Track form submissions and calls
20. Request Google reviews consistently

Mini SOP:
- Start with homepage, service pages, and GBP consistency.
- Fix titles, headings, and CTAs first.
- Then improve speed, internal links, and review collection.
- Publish local content after core pages are solid.

Use case:
Ideal for clinics, salons, coaches, consultants, gyms, interior designers, and local service businesses in India.
